Welcome to Rescue of the Blackthorn Clan, the first in Urban Realms' series of Date Night Dungeons.

My husband, Tom, and I have been playing role-playing games together for twenty years. We get together
with friends around a table, or a computer, and enjoy a slice of fantasy and adventure. But in all those years
we've never come across a game specifically made for couples to play together. We wanted something
where both of us could be players and enjoy the unfolding story, not where one knew all that would happen
and spelled out the story for the other. So we created Date Night Dungeons.

The idea is that each partner is both GM and player, and mysteries unfold for both as the story moves
forward. We've divided the adventure into four scenarios. Players are encouraged to switch GM duties for
each scenario and to not read past the current scenario to avoid spoilers. That way, both partners get to be
players, and the mystery lasts until the final scenario.

To facilitate the romance, we've also added a few extras, like Attraction Points, Turn-ons, and Pet Peeves.
These keep your mind focused on the romance as you move through the adventure. It's the dice, though,
that decide if our characters fall for each other or can't wait to get rid of each other. Then again, isn't love
always a roll of the dice?

So, light some candles, set the mood, and enjoy some mayhem together.

Catherine Thrush

Getting the Players Involved

Throughout the game, any time the players do the same
romantic action as the characters, they can decide together
how many bonus points to award. For example, if the
players hold hands when the characters hold hands, they
might decide it is worth +2 Attraction Points to each PC no
matter the dice rolls.
